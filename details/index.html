How to get there

Two hops: fly to Athens, then catch a ferry across the Aegean to Syros.

Book ferry tickets

Heads up: the ferry port isn't in central Athens. Athens airport to Piraeus port usually takes 45–75 minutes by taxi, and longer by public transport. Allow at least 1 hour (often more) to reach it from the airport or city — so don't book the ferry too close to your flight's arrival.

Where to stay 🏨

Base yourself in Ermoupoli

The main town of Syros — the most convenient spot, and the best base for spending time together before and after the wedding. A hotel or Airbnb here both work perfectly. We are staying at Apollonion Palace Hotel.

Open Apollonion Palace in Google Maps

Staying in another village is totally fine too — just keep in mind you'll likely need a taxi, car, or scooter 🚕 🛵 to get around.
